## STRATEGIC NOTES FOR GEORGE

**Why these questions work better:**

| Before | After | Why it's better |
|---|---|---|
| "What matters most on your verified reputation portfolio?" | "If you could show agencies one thing — what would it be?" | Grade 3 language vs. jargon |
| "Would you pay for premium features?" | Removed | Too early to ask — they don't know the product |
| "Have you experienced reputational opacity?" | "Have you ever lost a job because you couldn't prove your experience?" | Grade 3 language — everyone has an answer |
| "What would portability mean to your career sovereignty?" | "Would being able to take your reputation everywhere change how in control you feel?" | Simple, relatable |
| No intro | 3-sentence explainer + founding caregiver promise | They know why they're here and what they get |

**What was removed:**
- All questions about paying for premium (too early)
- Questions using words like "portability", "reputation system", "credentials", "verified"
- Demographics section (goes in post-signup survey)
- "Unasked question" field (too abstract)

**What was added:**
- Simple intro with founding caregiver promise
- Email capture framed as reward (early access + founding caregiver list)
- Closing message that makes them feel like insiders
